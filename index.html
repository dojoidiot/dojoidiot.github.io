the cnft collection

a cnft is a code non fungible token. the cnft collection explores the questions - are cnfts feasible and what can you do with them? each cnft is designed to answer a particular question, and the answer to each question then leads to the development of the next question. search for the dojoidiot at https://zora.co to find the cnft collection.

the questions
